Muichiro Tokito

From Kimetsu No Yaiba

Breathing Style: Mist Breathing (霞の呼吸, Kasumi no Kokyū)

Occupation: Demon Slayer

Affiliation: Demon Slayer Corps

Age: 14

Height: 160 cm

Favorite Food: Furofuki Daikon (simmered daikon radish)

Family Background

Muichiro is the twin brother of Yuichiro Tokito. after both their parents died, the two brothers struggled to survive alone. Despite Yuichiro’s protective nature, he was ultimately killed in a demon attack, leaving Muichiro determined to become a Demon Slayer and honor his family’s memory.

Mist Breathing Techniques

  • First Form: Low Clouds, Distant Haze (壱ノ型 垂天遠霞, Ichi no kata: Suiren Enka) – A powerful thrust that conceals Muichiro’s movements within mist.
  • Second Form: Eight-Layered Mist (弐ノ型 八重霞, Ni no kata: Yaegasumi) – A rapid slashing attack creating layers of mist to disorient the enemy.
  • Third Form: Scattering Mist Splash (参ノ型 霞散の飛沫, San no kata: Kasumi Shibuki) – Disperses mist around Muichiro to confuse his opponents and protect him.
  • Fourth Form: Shifting Flow Slash (肆ノ型 移流斬り, Shi no kata: Iryū Giri) – A swift movement that allows Muichiro to strike from unexpected angles.
  • Fifth Form: Sea of Clouds and Haze (伍ノ型 霞の海, Go no kata: Kasumi no Umi) – Envelops the area in dense mist, obscuring Muichiro’s presence.
  • Sixth Form: Lunar Dispersing Mist (陸ノ型 月の霞消, Roku no kata: Tsuki no Kasumi) – A sweeping slash attack that disperses mist, resembling moonlight.
  • Seventh Form: Obscuring Clouds (漆ノ型 朧, Shichi no kata: Oboro) – Muichiro’s ultimate form, in which he becomes nearly invisible, attacking with unmatched speed and precision.

History

After his family’s tragic loss, Muichiro sought purpose within the Demon Slayer Corps. Over time, he mastered Mist Breathing and became one of the elite Hashira, protecting others from suffering the same fate. His journey is a testament to resilience and dedication.

Trivia

  • Muichiro's favorite food is furofuki daikon, a traditional simmered daikon dish.
  • Despite his quiet demeanor, he cares deeply for others.
  • He finds peace in misty, mountainous areas, where he often meditates.
